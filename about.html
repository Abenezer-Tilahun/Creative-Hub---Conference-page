SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS ETHIOPIA SUMMIT 2022

The Creative Hub Ethiopia initiated by UNIDO and FeSMMIPA with funding from the Italian Development Cooperation. Iceaddis and African Mosaique are implementing partners.We offer a wide range of services to designers and innovative entrepreneurs mainly to help reinforce and enhance creativity through various focused and thematic technical support, innovation seminars and workshops, training, prototyping techniques, social events, and networking sessions. The Hub also has a fully equipped digital library, rapid prototyping services (e.g 3D printing), mock-up labs, meeting rooms, space for exhibition and events, co-working space, and cafeteria.
